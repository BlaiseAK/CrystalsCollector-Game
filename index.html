Currency Exchange

In the world you continually have different currencies changing in value compared to other countries currencies.

In this game you will be given 4 different currencies with different values every game you play. By clicking on one of the currency symbols you will add a specific amount of money to your "on hand cash."

You can win the game by matching your total amount of "on hand cash" to the computers random amount of money to buy a product.

The value of each currency symbol is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each currency.
